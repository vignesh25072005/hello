OUR PRODUCTS

Urea

Urea is a plant metabolite derived either from root uptake or from
catabolism of arginine by arginase. In agriculture, urea is intensively
used as a nitrogen fertilizer. Urea nitrogen enters the plant either directly,
or in the form of ammonium or nitrate after urea degradation by soil microbes.

Rs.270

Glyphosate


Glyphosate is a widely used herbicide that can kill certain weeds and grasses.
Glyphosate works by blocking an enzyme essential for plant growth.
The product is used primarily in agriculture, but also in forestry and lawn and garden care.

Rs.350

Acephate

Acephate is a general-use insecticide registered for use on food crops,
